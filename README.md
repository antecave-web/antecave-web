# Andreas Lidström, Ph.D.

Cognitive neuroscientist, analyst, and researcher with experience in longitudinal data analysis, behavioral science, neuroimaging, research methodology, and evidence-based decision support.

My work combines quantitative analysis, scientific rigor, and practical problem-solving. I have worked with complex datasets, longitudinal studies, statistical modeling, and project development in academic environments with high standards for ethics, data protection, and research quality.

## Areas of Expertise

* Public health and cognitive learning and aging
